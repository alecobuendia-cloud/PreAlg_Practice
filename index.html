Every file follows the same simple structure:

  3. Check you work: After you work through each guide we can meet to go through the answers!

You multiply their individual probabilities together: \(P(A \text{ and } B) = P(A) \times P(B)\).

2.1 Quick Formula Reference

Topic Formula
Mean \(\text{mean} = \dfrac{\text{sum of all values}}{\text{number of values}}\)
Median (even count) \(\text{median} = \dfrac{a + b}{2}\)
Range \(\text{range} = \text{largest value} - \text{smallest value}\)
Two overlapping groups \(\text{total} = (\text{group 1}) + (\text{group 2}) - (\text{both}) + (\text{neither})\)
Independent events \(P(A \text{ and } B) = P(A) \times P(B)\)
Probability without replacement \(P(A \text{ then } B) = P(A) \times P(B \mid A \text{ happened})\)
Circle area \(A = \pi r^2\)
Circle circumference \(C = 2\pi r\)
Sector area \(\text{sector area} = \dfrac{\text{angle}}{360} \times \pi r^2\)
Arc length \(\text{arc length} = \dfrac{\text{angle}}{360} \times 2\pi r\)
